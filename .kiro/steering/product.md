# Product Overview

Rice Trading App is a Progressive Web App (PWA) for buying and selling rice commodities. It installs on any phone like a native app — no app store needed. Traders, buyers, and suppliers can manage listings, negotiate prices, and execute trades.

## Core Capabilities

1. **Dashboard** — Market snapshot, recent orders/trades, quick action buttons
2. **Market** — Live price charts per variety (7/14/30/90 day), % change indicators
3. **Listings** — Browse/search/filter rice listings, create new listings
4. **Orders** — Place BUY/SELL orders with auto-matching engine, track fill progress
5. **Trades** — Full history with buyer/seller role, complete/dispute actions
6. **Profile** — Stats, editable info, sign out
7. **PWA** — Installable, works offline, mobile-first responsive design

## Target Users

- Rice traders and brokers
- Buyers (wholesalers, retailers, food manufacturers)
- Suppliers and farmers
- Personal use for tracking stock and transactions

## Key Principles

- Accurate, up-to-date pricing information
- Transparent trade history and order status
- Simple, efficient workflows — feels like a native app
- Works on any device with a browser — no download required
