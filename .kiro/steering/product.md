# Product Overview

Rice Trading Inventory System is a simple yet scalable solution designed to help manage, track stock, monitor transactions, and manage capital expenses.  

## Core Capabilities

1. Dashboard (Summary View)
    Total current stock (in sacks)
    Total stock in (purchases)
    Total stock out (sales)
    Remaining inventory balance
    Total capital invested
    Total revenue 
    Low stock alerts
2. Stock In/Out Management
    Date of transaction
    Type: Stock In / Stock Out
    Supplier / Customer name
    Rice type (e.g., Binhi, Jasmine, Local)
    Quantity (sacks)
    Price per sack
    Total amount
    Remarks (optional)
3. Inventory Tracking
    Running balance of stock per rice type
    Automatic calculation of remaining inventory
    Categorization per product type
4. Capital & Expenses Tracking
    Purchase costs (rice stock)
    Logistics (delivery, trucking)
    Miscellaneous expenses
    Total capital used
5. Reporting
    Daily / Weekly / Monthly summaries
    Stock movement reports
    Expense reports
    Profit tracking (if sales included)

## Target Users

- personal use

## Key Principles

- Transparent trade history and order status
- Simple, efficient workflows for common trading actions
